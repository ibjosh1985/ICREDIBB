..::|| Theming For iBB Gurus ||::..



.:| Editting Themes |:.
Note: The theme folder you wish to edit must be chmod 755 if on Unix

1) Go to the AdminCP

2) Click "Edit Theme Colors"

3) Choose the name of the color theme you wish to change.

4) Edit the colors to meet your needs.

5) Submit the editted theme.


.:| Creating New Themes |:.
Are you up to the challenge? 
You better be...
Because...
This isn't going to be easy...
Well, maybe :).

1) Go to the AdminCP.

2) Click "Create A New Color Theme".

3) Choose the name of your new color theme.

4) Choose the colors for you new theme. There is a ton of options, 
that is why this is only recommended for patient people with lots of 
time. :D

5) Submit the new theme.

6) Back in the AdminCP, click "Change Color Theme".

7) Choose your new theme you just spent tons of time working on.

8) Upload your special images to the images folder inside the theme folder.
